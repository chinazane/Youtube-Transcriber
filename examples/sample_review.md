## Grammar Points

### Polite vs. Plain Forms
- Use お〜さん (o-~san) when talking about other people's family
- Use plain forms when talking about your own family
- Example: お母さん (others' mother) vs. 母 (my mother)

---

## Study Tips

1. Practice polite/plain distinctions for family vocabulary
2. Repeat each word along with the teacher's pronunciation
3. Use timestamps to review specific vocabulary
